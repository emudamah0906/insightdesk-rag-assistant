# Shipping & Hardware (IoT add-on)

## Delivery times
Hardware sensors ship within 2 business days. Standard delivery is 5–7 business days;
express is 2 business days. We ship to the US, Canada, UK, and EU.

## Tracking
A tracking number is emailed when your order ships. Track from Orders → Order Detail.
If tracking has not updated in 5 days, contact support for a replacement.

## Returns and warranty
Hardware has a 1-year warranty covering manufacturing defects. Return unopened hardware
within 30 days for a full refund. Opened hardware can be exchanged if defective.

## Installation
Sensors pair via Bluetooth using the mobile app. Each sensor needs Wi-Fi and a power
source. See the in-app setup guide; most installs take under 10 minutes.

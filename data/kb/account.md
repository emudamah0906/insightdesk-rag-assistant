# Account & Security

## Account ownership
The account owner is the email used at signup. Ownership can be transferred from
Settings → Team → Transfer Ownership; the new owner must confirm via email.

## Security and privacy
All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256). We are SOC 2 Type II
certified. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) from Settings → Security. We never
sell customer data and do not use customer content to train third-party models.

## Roles and permissions
Roles are Owner, Admin, Member, and Viewer. Only Owners and Admins can change billing
or invite users. Viewers have read-only access.

## Deleting an account
Account deletion is permanent and removes all data after a 14-day grace period.
Request deletion from Settings → Account → Delete Account.
